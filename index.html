Facts about blood needs

  • Every two seconds someone needs blood.
  • More than 38,000 blood donations are needed every day.
  • A total of 30 million blood components are transfused each year.
  • The blood type most often requested by hospitals is Type O.

Blood Supply Facts

  • Type O-negative blood (red cells) can be transfused to patients of all blood types. It is always in great demand and often in short supply.
  • Type AB-positive plasma can be transfused to patients of all other blood types. AB plasma is also usually in short supply.

Blood donation process

  • Donating blood is a safe process. A sterile needle is used for each patient.
  • Blood donation is a simple four-step process: registration, medical history and mini-physical, donation and refreshments.
  • The actual blood donation typically takes less than 10-12 minutes.

Facts about blood and its components

  • Blood makes up about 7 percent of your body's weight.
  • There are four types of transfusable products that can be derived from blood: red cells, platelets, plasma and cryoprecipitate. Typically, two or three of these are produced from a unit of donated whole blood – hence each donation can help save up to three lives.
  • Donors can give either whole blood or specific blood components only. The process of donating specific blood components – red cells, plasma or platelets – is called apheresis.
  • One transfusion dose of platelets can be obtained through one apheresis donation of platelets or by combining the platelets derived from five whole blood donations.
  • Donated platelets must be used within five days of collection.
  • Healthy bone marrow makes a constant supply of red cells, plasma and platelets. The body will replenish the elements given during a blood donation – some in a matter of hours and others in a matter of weeks.


About Website

Drop of Life is website developed for finding blood donors in locality of the patient. The application provides an effective means for finding potential blood donors of required blood group in the vicinity of the area entered by the user. The application works by maintaining a database of the individuals who show willingness to donate blood by registering to the website. Details such as blood group, address and contact information are uploaded to the website by the potential donors the given details are then used to connect the person in need of blood to the donor.

The website is made to be user friendly and very easy to use. The individual just has to enter the required blood group and location and the list of individuals with their contact details who are willing to donate blood in the vicinity are shown to the user in need of blood. The user can then contact the donors and get the help that they need. If there are no donors available near the entered location then there is an option for posting the request on the website notification panel so as to increase the chances of getting the help that they need.
